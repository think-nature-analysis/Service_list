Our Actions Based on Nature and Biodiversity Big Data


ThinkNature inc. is a "researcher's startup" of nature data scientists. We propose solutions for sustainable relationship with biodiversity and ecosystem services through cutting-edge data analytics.
Our strengths are our group of researchers with outstanding achievements and the largest biodiversity big data in Japan. Our mission is "Nature Transformation of the future society".
