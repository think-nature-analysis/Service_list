Softscapes for the Net Gain

Biodiversity as natural capital is the root of sustainability of our society. Private companies are required to take effective actions toward nature-positive activities to restore the nature.
TN GAIN takes a scientific approach using AI and big data to quantify the impact of corporate activities on nature from the perspective of biodiversity. It can be utilized in PR and IR activities, as well as in the creation of KPIs for nature restoration activities.

INNOVATIONS in TN GAIN

Determination of native species

Based on high-resolution tree species distribution data, we determine if a candidate tree species for planting is native to the area.

Predict the diversity of animals in your garden

Matching bird and butterfly food habits database with distribution data, we list species that visit your gardens.

Calculate optimal species composition of planting tree

Providing locations and areas of your garden, we provides the most effective combination of tree species for biodiversity restoration.

Biodiversity in Business

In recent years, the movement to conserve and regenerate biodiversity has accelerated around the world: the 15th Conference of the Parties (COP15) to the Convention on Biological Diversity was held in December 2022, and 23 targets to be achieved by 2030 were adopted as the Kunming-Montreal Biodiversity Framework. For example, 30by30, which aims to establish at least 30% of land and sea as nature reserves, and the promotion of biodiversity impact assessment and information disclosure in business activities (e.g., TNFD). All of these goals are issues that Japan, a biodiversity hotspot, is taking the lead in addressing.
