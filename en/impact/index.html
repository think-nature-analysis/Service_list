TN IMPACT

Fine scale natural capital visualization based on remote- and ground-sensed biodiversity data and machine learning.

Quantitatively assessing development impact and postive effects of nature-friendly actions.

Toward the Future of LIVING IN HARMONY WITH NATURE

Fine resolution everywhere

The dynamics of biodiversity at local scales are influenced both by macroecological processes operating at broader scales and by land use at the sub-hectare level. TN IMPACT is a GLOBALLY AVAILABLE tool that integrates satellite imagery, environmental variables, and observational data on species and ecosystem services at ultra-high resolution through hierarchical modeling, enabling the visualization, prediction, and optimization of the impact of human activities on natural capital.

INNOVATIONS in TN IMPACT

Real-Time Insights into Human Impacts on Biodiversity

Modeling that integrates satellite imagery, regional species pools, and environmental variables captures both broad-scale and local-scale processes of biodiversity. Future projections based on satellite imagery are updated in near real-time, allowing quantitative tracking of biodiversity changes driven by human activities.

Reconstructing the Past and Projecting the Future

By reconstructing past natural conditions through modeling, it becomes possible to evaluate accumulated contributions to nature. When adapted to the future, the model enables numerical assessment of potential biodiversity gains.
